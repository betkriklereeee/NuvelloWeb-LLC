We start with a discovery call to understand your business, goals, and audience. From there we lock in the sitemap and feature list, move into design (reviewed and approved by you before we write a line of code), then development, QA, and launch. Post-launch we provide 30 days of included support so you are never left hanging.

Most projects follow a clean 6-step flow: Discovery → Design → Development → QA → Launch → Grow.

SEO

SEO is a long game. Most businesses start seeing measurable movement in rankings and traffic within 3 to 6 months. Competitive markets or brand-new domains can take longer. Anyone who promises you page-one rankings in 30 days is either lying or using tactics that will get your site penalized.

We are honest about timelines and we track everything so you can see exactly what is happening month over month.

No — and you should be very suspicious of anyone who does. Google controls rankings, not us. What we guarantee is that we will follow best practices, build your authority the right way, and give your site the best possible chance to rank. We track performance transparently and adjust strategy when needed.

Local SEO focuses on getting your business to appear in searches tied to a specific geographic area — things like “web designer near me” or “plumber in Orange County.” It involves Google Business Profile optimization, local citations, and location-specific content. Regular (organic) SEO focuses on ranking for broader, non-location-specific terms. Most local businesses need both.

Yes. Ads stop the moment you stop paying. SEO builds compounding, long-term visibility that keeps working even when you are not running campaigns. The two work best together — ads give you immediate traffic while SEO builds the foundation that reduces your cost-per-lead over time.

Depending on the engagement it can include keyword research, technical site audit and fixes, on-page optimization, content strategy, local SEO, Google Business Profile management, link building, and monthly reporting. We build a custom strategy based on your goals — not a one-size-fits-all package.
